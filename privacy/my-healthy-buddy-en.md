# Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information concerning users of My Healthy Buddy is processed and protected.

- Application: **My Healthy Buddy**
- Age rating: **Everyone**
- Effective date: **April 8, 2026**

DevOni (hereinafter referred to as the “Service Provider”) establishes and publishes this Privacy Policy in compliance with the Personal Information Protection Act and other applicable laws and regulations when providing the My Healthy Buddy mobile application. The protection of the personal information of children under the age of 14 is our highest priority.

This Privacy Policy applies to the My Healthy Buddy application.

## 1. Purposes of Processing Personal Information

The Service Provider processes personal information for the following purposes. Personal information being processed will not be used for any purpose other than those listed below. If a purpose changes, the Service Provider will take the measures required by applicable laws and regulations.

- Providing and operating an application service intended for children
- Analyzing errors and providing a stable service
- Improving service quality and responding to security issues
- Responding to customer inquiries and handling disputes and complaints

## 2. Categories of Personal Information Processed

### 1) Information Required for Account Registration

- Email address (login and account recovery)
- Password (member authentication)
- Nickname
- Phone number
- Gender
- BACKND member unique identifier (automatically generated during account registration)

The above information is stored on the BACKND server for account registration and management, login, account recovery, and provision of user profiles.

### 2) Information Provided Directly by Users

- Email address and details of an inquiry provided when contacting customer support

## 3. Processing of Children's Personal Information

This application is primarily intended for children under the age of 14, and protecting children's personal information is our highest priority.

The Service Provider processes personal information according to the following standards to protect children's personal information.

- **Data minimization:** Only the minimum personal information that is strictly necessary to provide the service is collected.
- **Data protection and prohibition on use beyond the stated purpose:** A UUID collected automatically for internal identification is used solely to provide the service. It is not used for personal identification, advertising, marketing, or external analytics. It is also not disclosed externally or used to enable third parties to collect behavioral information.
- **Rights of legal representatives:** A legal representative of a child under the age of 14 has the right to request access to, correction or deletion of, or suspension of the processing of the child's personal information.
- **How to submit a request:** If a legal representative wishes to request deletion of a child's data or exercise another right, they may contact the Privacy Officer using the email address provided below. The Service Provider will verify the requester's identity and take action without undue delay.
- **Lawful consent:** Where the consent of a legal representative is required, personal information is collected and used only after obtaining consent through legally compliant procedures.

## 4. Processing and Retention Periods

The Service Provider retains and uses personal information according to the following standards.

### 1. Account Registration and Profile Information

- **Categories processed:** Email address, password, nickname, phone number, gender, and BACKND member unique identifier
- **Purpose of retention:** Account registration and management, login, account recovery, and provision of user profiles
- **Retention period:** Until account withdrawal

### 2. Personal Information Collected Through Customer Inquiries

- **Purpose of retention:** To respond to and resolve customer inquiries, improve the service, and handle disputes and complaints.
- **Retention period:** Until the inquiry has been resolved

Where retention is required under applicable laws and regulations, personal information will be retained for the period prescribed by those laws and regulations.

## 5. Procedures and Methods for Destroying Personal Information

The Service Provider destroys personal information without undue delay when it is no longer necessary, such as when the retention period has expired or the purpose of processing has been fulfilled.

### 1. Destruction Procedure

Personal information for which a reason for destruction has arisen is selected and destroyed in accordance with internal standards.

### 2. Destruction Methods

- Personal information stored in electronic files is permanently deleted using methods that prevent recovery.
- Personal information recorded on paper is destroyed by shredding or incineration.

## 6. Provision to Third Parties and Overseas Transfer

As a general rule, the Service Provider does not disclose children's personal information to external parties.

However, the following third-party service may be used to ensure stable operation of the application, and personal information may be transferred overseas in the process.

- **Recipient:** Google LLC (Google Play Services)
- **Destination country:** United States
- **Information transferred:** Device information and application usage records
- **Purpose of transfer:** Service provision and system operation
- **Time and method of transfer:** Automatically transmitted over a network when the application is launched

The Service Provider does not collect personal information for advertising or provide personalized advertising.

## 7. Rights of Data Subjects and Legal Representatives and How to Exercise Them

Users and the legal representatives of children may exercise the following rights at any time.

- Request access to personal information
- Request correction or deletion of personal information
- Request suspension of the processing of personal information
- Withdraw consent to the processing of personal information

These rights may be exercised by sending a request to the email address below. The Service Provider will take action without undue delay in accordance with applicable laws and regulations.

## 8. Measures to Safeguard Personal Information

The Service Provider implements the following measures to securely protect children's personal information.

- Minimizing access privileges for personal information
- Establishing and operating internal management standards
- Managing the security of personal information processing systems
