Privacy Policy

This policy explains how information is processed and protected when you use My Healthy Buddy.

DevOni (the “Service Provider”) establishes and publishes this Privacy Policy in compliance with the Personal Information Protection Act and other applicable laws when providing the My Healthy Buddy mobile application. Protecting the personal information of children under the age of 14 is a top priority.

This Privacy Policy applies to the My Healthy Buddy application.

Notice to parents and guardians: My Healthy Buddy is intended for children to use under the guidance and supervision of a parent, guardian, or other adult. An adult should assist the child when creating an account.

1. Purposes of Processing Personal Information

The Service Provider processes personal information for the following purposes. Personal information will not be used for purposes other than those listed below. If a purpose changes, the Service Provider will take the measures required by applicable laws.

  • Providing and operating an application service for children to use under adult guidance and supervision
  • Analyzing errors arising from service use and providing a stable service
  • Improving service quality and responding to security issues
  • Responding to customer inquiries and handling disputes and complaints

2. Categories of Personal Information Processed

1) Required fields on the account creation screen

  • Email address (for sign-in and account recovery)
  • Password (for account authentication)
  • Nickname
  • Phone number
  • Gender
  • BACKND member unique identifier (automatically generated when creating an account)

These fields are required on the account creation screen, but the Service Provider does not verify whether the values are true, whether they belong to the person entering them, or whether they match the user's actual information.

Users do not need to enter actual personal information in the nickname, phone number, or gender fields and may enter arbitrary values. Ownership of an email address is also not verified; however, account recovery may be limited if the user enters an address they cannot access. Parents, guardians, and users should avoid entering a real name, actual phone number, or other directly identifying information that is not necessary to use the service.

The values entered on the account creation screen are stored on BACKND servers to support account creation and management, sign-in, account recovery, and user profiles.

2) Information provided directly by users

  • Email address and inquiry details provided when contacting customer support

3. Processing Children's Personal Information

The application is primarily intended for children under the age of 14 and is designed on the premise that children use it under the guidance and supervision of a parent, guardian, or other adult, with an adult assisting in account creation.

The Service Provider processes children's personal information according to the following standards:

  • Adult guidance and supervision: A parent, guardian, or other adult should assist with account creation and service use and make sure the child does not enter unnecessary actual personal information.
  • No verification of entered information: Account creation fields are required, but the Service Provider does not verify whether the entered values are true, belong to the user, or match the user's actual information.
  • Data minimization: Only the minimum personal information unavoidably necessary to provide the service is collected.
  • Data protection and prohibition of use for other purposes: A UUID collected automatically for internal identification is used only to provide the service. It is not used to personally identify users or for advertising, marketing, or external analytics. It is not disclosed externally or used to enable third parties to collect behavioral information.
  • Rights of legal representatives: A legal representative of a child under the age of 14 may request access to, correction or deletion of, or suspension of processing of the child's personal information.
  • How to submit a request: To request deletion of a child's data or exercise another right, the legal representative may contact the Privacy Officer at the email address below. The Service Provider will verify the requester's identity and act without undue delay.
  • Consent of a legal representative: Adult guidance and supervision, and the option to enter arbitrary values, do not replace any legally required consent process. When actual personal information of a child under the age of 14 must be processed on the basis of consent, the Service Provider obtains consent from the child's legal representative and verifies that consent.

4. Processing and Retention Period

The Service Provider retains and uses personal information according to the following standards:

1. Account registration and profile information

Information processed: The email address, password, nickname, phone number, and gender entered on the account creation screen, and the automatically generated BACKND member unique identifier (the Service Provider does not verify whether entered values are actual information)

Purpose of retention: Account creation and management, sign-in, account recovery, and providing user profiles

Retention period: Until the user closes the account

2. Personal information collected through customer inquiries

Purpose of retention: Responding to customer inquiries, resolving inquiries, improving the service, and handling disputes and complaints.

Retention period: Until the inquiry has been resolved

If applicable laws require information to be retained, it will be retained for the period prescribed by those laws.

5. Procedures and Methods for Destruction

When personal information is no longer necessary because its retention period has expired or its processing purpose has been achieved, the Service Provider destroys it without undue delay.

1. Destruction procedure

Personal information subject to destruction is selected and destroyed according to internal standards.

2. Destruction methods

Personal information stored electronically is permanently deleted using a method that prevents recovery.

Personal information recorded on paper is shredded or incinerated.

6. Third-Party Provision and Overseas Transfer

As a rule, the Service Provider does not provide children's personal information to outside parties.

However, the third-party service listed below may be used to provide the application reliably. Personal information may be transferred outside the Republic of Korea in this process.

  • Recipient: Google LLC (Google Play Services)
  • Destination country: United States
  • Information transferred: Device information and application usage records
  • Purpose of transfer: Service provision and system operation
  • Timing and method: Automatically transmitted over a network when the application is launched

The application does not collect personal information for advertising or provide personalized advertising.

7. Rights of Data Subjects and Legal Representatives

Users and the legal representatives of children may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Request access to personal information
  • Request correction or deletion of personal information
  • Request suspension of personal information processing
  • Withdraw consent to personal information processing

These rights may be exercised by sending an email to the contact address below. The Service Provider will respond without undue delay in accordance with applicable laws.

8. Measures to Protect Personal Information

The Service Provider takes the following measures to protect children's personal information:

  • Minimizing access privileges for personal information
  • Establishing and operating internal management standards
  • Managing the security of personal information processing systems

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be revised to reflect changes in applicable laws or the service. Changes will be announced in the application or on the published policy page.

This Privacy Policy is effective as of July 22, 2026.

11. User Consent

This Policy is a notice explaining how personal information is processed. A separate consent process applies whenever consent is required under applicable laws.

If processing the personal information of a child under the age of 14 requires the consent of a legal representative, use of the application or adult guidance and supervision alone is not treated as consent.
